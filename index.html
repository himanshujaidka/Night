    About Us

    We, the people of HMRITM family, feel delighted and privileged on successfully developing young minds into disciplined and responsible technocrats with strong foundation and high potential to shape the future. We are continuously working to fulfil the need of dynamic minds that can adapt to the changing scenario and complex situations in this highly competitive world. With a team of highly qualified, experienced, and truly dedicated faculties, we not only focus on teaching and learning but also aim toward the overall personality development of our students. The Training & Placements cell continuously guides and monitors the growth of students to fabricate their technical experience and leadership qualities. We are committed to provide industry ready professionals.

    Mission

    To Detect, Restore, Refine, Generate and Triumph. An epidemic is hardest for the people of a developing economy, particularly students. Standing amongst this chaos, volatility, and uncertainty; they look for educators who can guide them to a sustainable and worthy future. It is a challenge, and we are completely equipped to face it. With years of experience, world class facilities, renowned faculty team and cutting-edge, revolutionary research facilities; HMRITM ranks high in discovering, repairing and moulding a budding individual into a star of tomorrow.

    Vision

    To lead and support the technically bent community and empower them to become celebrated leaders; inculcate wisdom and values in them that would transform their individuality and life into a golden treasure of success, morality, and prosperity.
