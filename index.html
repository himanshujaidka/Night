    About Us

    We, the people of HMRITM family, feel delighted and privileged on successfully developing young minds into disciplined and responsible technocrats with strong foundation and high potential to shape the future. We are continuously working to fulfil the need of dynamic minds that can adapt to the changing scenario and complex situations in this highly competitive world. With a team of highly qualified, experienced, and truly dedicated faculties, we not only focus on teaching and learning but also aim toward the overall personality development of our students. The Training & Placements cell continuously guides and monitors the growth of students to fabricate their technical experience and leadership qualities. We are committed to provide industry ready professionals.

    Mission

    To Detect, Restore, Refine, Generate and Triumph. An epidemic is hardest for the people of a developing economy, particularly students. Standing amongst this chaos, volatility, and uncertainty; they look for educators who can guide them to a sustainable and worthy future. It is a challenge, and we are completely equipped to face it. With years of experience, world class facilities, renowned faculty team and cutting-edge, revolutionary research facilities; HMRITM ranks high in discovering, repairing and moulding a budding individual into a star of tomorrow.

    Vision

    To lead and support the technically bent community and empower them to become celebrated leaders; inculcate wisdom and values in them that would transform their individuality and life into a golden treasure of success, morality, and prosperity.

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Q. Why should I recruit from HMR ITM?

      Every year, more than 500 talented students pass out of HMRITM. They enter various fields and excel in every role they take up. Along with this, some of the best companies recruit talent on-campus. Your organization is guaranteed to find students fit for the roles you offer, be it MBA students, interns or Engineers.

    • Q. What sort of companies visit HMR ITM?

      Companies of all domains visit HMRITM. Whether it is consulting firms, PSU’s, Electronics companies or companies in the core engineering fields and startup.

    • Q. What are the courses offered at HMRITM and what is the student strength available for placement?

      The courses at HMRITM include B.Tech. in over 6 branches ranging from Computer Science & Engineering to Mechanical & Automation Engineering. Along with this HMRITM offers post graduation courses in MBA. You can request for more details atContacts.

    • Q. I want to visit HMRITM for recruitment, how should I go about it?

      All you have to do is get in touch with one of our Placements Coordinators by sending in an email describing your company, role being offered and compensation to students. They will get in touch with you and tell you all about the further process. You can find contact details atContacts.

    • Q. How can I reach the HMRITM?

      HMRITM is situated in Hamidpur. The area surrounding it is Bakoli. Once you have finalised the dates of visit, our placement coordinators can also send you the location of the College. This makes it easy to take a cab to the College. If you are travelling by metro, you can reach at Jahangir Puri. From there you can take cabs or autos to reach Bakoli and then to the College. The locals are well acquainted with the College’s location and once near Bakoli, the sign boards make it easy to find the College.

      • Q. How Can I be in contact with Training & Placement Cell of HMR ITM?

      You can call or mail on the details at:Contacts.
